### How to Pick the Perfect Gift

1. **Know Their Stage**  
   - *Vinyl lover?* Go for a rare pressing or a turntable.  
   - *DJ in the making?* A controller or a home‑studio kit.  
   - *Fashion‑savvy?* Streetwear or a custom tee.  
   - *History buff?* A well‑researched book or documentary.  

2. **Consider Their Space**  
   - If they have a small apartment, a portable speaker or a vinyl cleaning kit might be more practical than a full‑size turntable.  

3. **Add a Personal Touch**  
   - Handwritten notes, custom artwork, or a playlist you’ve curated can turn a great gift into a memorable one.  

4. **Budget‑Friendly Options**  
   - A mixtape USB, a vinyl cleaning kit, or a custom lyric poster can be under $30 and still feel special.  

5. **Experiences Over Things**  
   - Tickets to a concert or a dance class can create lasting memories and are often more appreciated than a physical item.
